Game of Zones

A social exploration game, with a health tracker!



Game of Zones is a Node.js webapp that pits users against each other in a battle of dominance. Players can run around landmark buildings in UC San Diego to capture them for their team. As players play the game, various stats such as distance traveled and calories burned are tracked. Ideally, you'd play the game naturally as the day goes on by taking alternate paths from class to class, but players can also intentionally go for a run to score for their team.

This webapp was a quarter-long project with a team of 2. We used MongoDB as a database to store user data, and Node.js as a frontend. We continually iterated on our design by applying needfinding and paper prototype at the start, with heuristic evaluation and A/B testing, and Google Analytics later on.
